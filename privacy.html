GUSTUS APP PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 15.12.2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Maximilian Kallina (“Developer”, “We”, “Us”, or “Our”) collects, uses, and protects the information provided by users (“You” or “User”) of the Gustus mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Data Collected Initially

At this stage of beta testing, no personal data or usage data is being collected from Users. The App is currently focused on functionality testing and feedback gathering without tracking or storing any personal information.

1.2 Potential Data Collection in the Future

In future updates, we may collect the following types of data to improve the App’s performance and user experience:

  • Crash Reports: We may collect crash data to diagnose errors and improve the stability of the App.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data on how Users interact with the App, including screen navigation, feature usage, and session length.
  • Device Information: We may collect technical data about the device used to run the App, such as the operating system, device model, and app version.

Legal Basis for Data Processing

Any future data processing will be based on the following legal grounds according to Art. 6 GDPR:

  • Your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • Performance of contract or pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
  • Compliance with legal obligations (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR)
  • Our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR)

Data Transfer to Third Countries

Currently, no data is transferred to third countries. If this changes in the future, we will implement appropriate safeguards according to Chapter V of the GDPR.

2. How We Use Collected Data

If data collection is introduced in future versions, the information we collect will be used solely for the following purposes:

  • To identify and fix technical issues (e.g., crash logs)
  • To improve the App’s performance and user experience
  • To analyze general usage patterns to enhance features and functionalities

3. How We Protect Your Information

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Any data we collect in the future will be stored and processed in compliance with data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) if applicable. We will implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard the data.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or in connection with improving the App (e.g., with analytics services). If any third-party services are used, we will update this policy to inform you about such sharing.

5. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding any personal data we may collect in the future:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: You can request correction of any inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: You can request that your data be deleted.
  • Objection: You can object to data collection for certain purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at maximilian@kallina.at.

Automated Decision Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling according to Article 22 GDPR.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as necessary. Any changes will be posted within the App, and we will notify you of significant updates. Your continued use of the App after such updates constitutes acceptance of the changes.
